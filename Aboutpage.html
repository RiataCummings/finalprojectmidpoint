Who We Are

Founded in 2018 by Korby Christiansen, Under the Swell is a custom leatherwork provider. We are dedicated to providing quality leatherwork at an affordable price. Our beautifully handcrafted products are designed to fill both form and function for our customers. We are based out of Emery, Utah, and we understand the importance of having tack you can depend on and accessories that never fail. We have a passion for quality, and we take pride in everything we make.The things that we make can become useful tools, beautiful accessories, and even pricelees heirlooms.

Leatherwork isn't the only thing we care about, though. Our home near the San Rafael Swell has given us an appreciation for agriculture and the western way of life. In each of our products and in all of our dealings, we strive to represent agriculturalist in the best possible way and to promote the western way of life. Our founder and our team strive to be responsible, hardworking, caring, and fair. We are just a small, family business trying to make a difference in the lives of as many people as we can, and we appreciate every customer.

What We Do

Under the Swell produces custom and stock leatherwork at great prices. We also have a new and expanding apparel line. We take custom orders for our leather products, and strive to create the best for each of our customers. When our employees aren't working in the leather shop, they are actively engaged in the western way of life. They opperate ranches and horse training businesses. They compete in high school, college, and professional rodeo. They raise the next generation of cowboys and cowgirls. We love what we do, and it shows in our work.

Where We Are

We operate out of a tiny little farm town in Utah, in a place called Emery. To are west are the beautiful Manti La Sal Mountains where we run our cattle. To our east are the deserts of the San Rafael. To us, this is the most beautiful place in the world.

Why We Do This

Our mission is to provide our customers with beautiful leather products of the highest caliber. Working with leather connects us to our western heritage and gives us an avenue to share that love with others. By carving and tooling attractive designs into leather, we extend the leathers life and give it a purpose. We find joy in the effort, and hope to bring you joy with the product of our toil.
